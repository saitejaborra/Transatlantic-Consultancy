About the USA

From the Big Easy to the Big Apple, the United States is truly the land of opportunities.

The USA is home to the highest number of international students in the world.

With famous cities, highly ranked universities and exciting campus environments, studying in the USA offers the perfect blend of educational quality and cultural experience.

Why study in the USA ?

Thanks to the sheer size of the country and the large number of universities and schools, there is an institution suited for every student in the USA. With liberal arts colleges on an intimate scale to huge state campuses and leading research-driven universities, you will find your own niche here.

People and culture

USA is famous for its pristine technological advancements. Rich history and diverse blend of people and culture makes your life amazing.

Education system in the USA

The American college experience is unlike any other. Host to world-class universities and unique specialist institutions, there are a broad range of study options for undergraduate and postgraduate students from around the world

Higher education in the US is divided into three main types of institutions:

  • junior or community colleges
  • vocational schools
  • universities or colleges
